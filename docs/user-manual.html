1. System Overview

1.1 About this Manual

This manual is the official reference guide for the Eltuff Ideas Ventures Business Portal â€” a web-based management platform designed and developed by Nexacore Innovations (nexacore-innovations.com). It covers every module available to Admin, Staff, and Client users.

The portal is accessible from any modern web browser on desktop or mobile. No software installation is required.

1.2 Platform Architecture

The platform is built on a secure cloud infrastructure with role-based access control (RBAC). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The portal is divided into two primary areas:

Staff Portal STAFF ADMIN

Full business management: quotes, invoices, orders, clients, inventory, production, finance, reports, and settings.

Client Portal CLIENT

Self-service view of orders, invoices, reports, and account settings. Read-only on most data.

1.3 User Roles & Permissions

There are three user roles in the system. Each role controls which pages and actions a user can access:

Role Who it's for Portal Access Key Capabilities
ADMIN Business owner / manager Staff Portal + Settings Full access to all data, promote/demote staff, view all reports, manage system settings
STAFF Employees, operators Staff Portal Create & manage quotes, invoices, orders, clients, inventory, production, expenses; view reports
CLIENT Business customers Client Portal View own orders & invoices, download reports, update account profile
A user's role is assigned by an Admin from the Settings â†’ Staff Accounts page. Clients register themselves and are automatically assigned the Client role.

4.1 Creating a Quote

  1. Click the New Quote button (top-right of the Quotes page).
  2. Select the Client from the dropdown (all registered clients are listed).
  3. Add line items using the + Add Item button. For each item enter: description, quantity, and unit price. The line total updates automatically.
  4. Set the Tax % (e.g. 15 for VAT), Valid Until date, and optional notes.
  5. The Subtotal / Tax / Total preview updates live at the bottom of the dialog.
  6. Click Create Quote. A unique quote number (e.g. QT-2026-A3F) is assigned automatically.

4.2 Managing Quotes & Statuses

  1. Click the eye icon (ğŸ‘) on any quote row to open the Quote Detail dialog.
  2. Inside the detail view, use the quick action buttons to update status: Mark Sent, Accept, Reject, Mark Expired.
  3. The status change is saved instantly and the quote list refreshes.

4.3 Converting a Quote to Invoice

  1. Open an Accepted quote's detail view.
  2. Click the Convert to Invoice button.
  3. The system creates a new invoice with all line items copied from the quote. The quote status is automatically updated to Accepted and linked.
  4. You are redirected to the Invoices page where the new invoice appears with Draft status, ready to edit and send.
A quote can only be converted once. The button disappears after conversion to prevent duplicates.

B. Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, click Forgot password? and enter your email address. You will receive a password reset link. The link expires after 1 hour â€” request a new one if it expires.

I can't see the Staff Portal after logging in.

Your account may be set to the Client role. Ask an Admin to promote your account to Staff from the Settings â†’ Staff Accounts tab.

A raw material stock level is wrong after a batch.

Stock is deducted automatically when a batch is Confirmed. If the level is still wrong, an Admin can manually edit the stock quantity from the Inventory page.

Can I edit an invoice after it has been sent?

Yes â€” invoices can be edited at any status except Paid. Open the invoice detail view, click Edit, make your changes, and save. The client will see the updated version if they refresh.

Why is the Convert to Invoice button missing on a quote?

The button only appears on quotes with Accepted status, and disappears after the quote has already been converted once. Change the status to Accepted first if it hasn't been set.

How do I change the VAT rate applied to new invoices?
